PocketAI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024-08-21

Introduction

PocketAI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use the PocketAI application ("PocketAI", "App", or "Service"). By using PocketAI, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Email address
  • Calendar data
  • User-created AI personality data
  • Chat logs and interaction history

Google User Data

PocketAI accesses and processes the following Google user data:

  • Gmail: We can only access your Gmail account to send emails on your behalf. We do not read, store, or process any other email content beyond what is necessary to fulfill your specific requests.
  • Google Calendar: We view and edit events on your calendar on your behalf. This data is accessed only to manage events per your instructions and is not retained or shared beyond its immediate use for the requested action.
  • Google Docs: We can see, edit, create, and delete your Google Docs documents at your request. We only access and modify documents as explicitly instructed by you.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information and Google user data to:

  1. Provide and maintain our Service
  2. Personalize your AI assistant experience
  3. Communicate with you about our Service
  4. Comply with legal obligations

Long-term Memory Feature

PocketAI's unique long-term memory feature allows your AI assistant to remember important details, experiences, and past conversations. This information is stored securely and is used solely to enhance your personal AI experience. The long-term memory data is processed and stored as embeddings, ensuring that the original content is not directly accessible.

Data Storage and Security

PocketAI employs industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. We use encryption and secure protocols to transmit and store your data. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery.

We use trusted third-party service providers to enhance our services and ensure the secure processing of your data:

  • Data Storage: We store your personal and interaction data securely with third-party cloud service providers that comply with industry-standard security practices, including encryption and access controls.
  • AI Data Management: To deliver personalized AI experiences, we use third-party services to manage and store AI-related data, ensuring this information is kept secure and private.
  • Payment Processing: We use a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor to securely handle your payment information. We do not store credit card details on our servers; all transactions are processed by our payment provider.

These third-party providers are bound by strict data protection agreements to ensure your data is processed in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with our Services and as described in this Privacy Policy. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings. Upon request, your account and data will be deleted within 30 days, unless retention is required by law.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may only share your information with law enforcement or other governmental agencies if required by law.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@pocketaiassistant.com or use the relevant features in the app settings.

Explicit Consent

By using PocketAI and connecting your Google account, you give explicit consent for PocketAI to access and process your Google data as described in this policy. This consent is obtained through the OAuth 2.0 protocol when you link your Google account to PocketAI.

Children's Privacy

PocketAI is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Compliance with Legal Obligations

PocketAI complies with applicable data protection regulations, including GDPR where applicable. Users have the right to lodge complaints with their local data protection authorities regarding the processing of their personal data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page, updating the "Last Updated" date, and, for significant changes, providing a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of Privacy Policy changes).

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@pocketaiassistant.com.

Compliance with Google's Limited Use Requirements

PocketAI's use of Google user data complies with Google's Limited Use requirements. We only use this data to provide or improve user-facing features that are prominent in the requesting application's user interface. We do not transfer this data to others unless necessary to provide or improve these features, to comply with applicable laws, or as part of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets with notice to users. We do not use this data for serving advertisements or for any other purposes.

By using PocketAI, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to these terms, you must not use PocketAI.
